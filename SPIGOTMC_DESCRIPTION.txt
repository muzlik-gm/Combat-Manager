[SIZE=6][B]What is True Combat Manager?[/B][/SIZE]

True Combat Manager is a comprehensive combat management plugin for competitive Minecraft servers. It tracks every aspect of player fights with detailed statistics, enforces intelligent restrictions to prevent unfair advantages, protects against combat logging, and presents everything through a beautiful, customizable interface.

Built for performance with async processing, intelligent lag compensation, and thread-safe architecture. Perfect for practice servers, factions, KitPvP, or any PvP-focused gameplay.

[B]Why Choose This Plugin?[/B]
‚Ä¢ Stop combat logging with instant-death protection and inventory drops
‚Ä¢ Prevent unfair escapes with smart cooldowns (pearls, gapples, elytra, teleports)
‚Ä¢ Track player performance with lifetime statistics (wins, losses, K/D, damage)
‚Ä¢ Safe zone protection prevents players from entering protected areas during combat
‚Ä¢ Visual glass barriers show at safezone boundaries
‚Ä¢ Creative mode protection automatically switches creative players to survival
‚Ä¢ Customize everything - 6 themes, 6 sound profiles, 100+ config options
